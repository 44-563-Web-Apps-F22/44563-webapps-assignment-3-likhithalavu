National Museum

Salar Jung Museum takes you on a nostalgic tour of the past, with its collection of royal trappings such as sculpture, paintings, textiles, manuscripts, ceramics, carpets, clocks, furniture etc all from various parts of the world.

Closest Airport to the Museum: Hyderabad (RGIA)

There are 5 ways to get from Delhi Airport to National Museum

  1. Bus
  2. Taxi
  3. Car
  4. Subway
  5. Towncar

There is no direct connection from Salar Jung Museum to Hyderabad Airport (RGIA). However, you can take the walk to Mahatma Gandhi Bus Station, take the subway to Lakdi-ka-pul, take the walk to AC Guards, then take the bus to Rajiv Gandhi International Airport. Alternatively, you can take the taxi to Hyderabad Airport (RGIA).
