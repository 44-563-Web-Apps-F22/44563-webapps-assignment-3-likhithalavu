Lockport Liger

The history of lion-tiger hybrids dates to at least the early 19th century in Lockport

  1. The liger has a faint tiger-like striped pattern upon a lionesque tawny background
  2. The liger is often believed to be the largest known cat in the world
  3. Ligers are about the same size as the prehistoric Smilodon populator and American lion.
  4. Ligers typically have a life expectancy of between 13 and 18 years
